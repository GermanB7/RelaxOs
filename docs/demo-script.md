# TranquiloOS Demo Script

Target length: 3-5 minutes.

## 1. Hook - 15 seconds

"Most people don't fail because of income, but because of poor decisions under pressure."

TranquiloOS is built around that idea. It is not a budgeting app. It is a personal decision system for independent living.

## 2. Problem - 30 seconds

When someone wants to move out, the question is rarely just "Can I pay rent?"

The real problem is a messy decision environment:

- Expenses are scattered.
- Rent looks affordable until transport, food, deposits, and home setup are included.
- Impulse purchases compete with essentials.
- People decide under stress without a system.
- A scenario can feel exciting but still be fragile.

## 3. Solution - 1 minute

TranquiloOS turns the move-out decision into a structured operating system.

The user models scenarios, adds expenses, calculates an independence score, sees risks, receives recommendations, organizes home setup purchases, activates an adaptive mode, evaluates transport options, and gets practical meal suggestions.

The key is that the backend owns the decision logic. The frontend does not guess the score, recommendation, scenario winner, or transport fit. It presents the system's reasoning and lets the user act.

## 4. Live Flow - 2 to 3 minutes

1. Start on the Dashboard.
   - Show current state, score, main risk, and next best action.
   - Explain that the dashboard is a command center, not a decorative chart page.

2. Open Scenarios.
   - Create or open a scenario.
   - Show income, emergency fund, and status.

3. Add Expenses.
   - Add rent, groceries, utilities, transport, and food delivery.
   - Explain that the system needs a realistic baseline, not perfect accounting.

4. Calculate Score.
   - Show the score, status, factors, and risks.
   - Point out rent burden, monthly margin, emergency coverage, and fixed burden.

5. Recalculate Recommendations.
   - Show the recommendation center.
   - Explain that recommendations are deterministic and tied to risk context.

6. Compare Scenarios.
   - Compare 2-4 scenarios.
   - Show that the system does not blindly pick the highest score if risk context is worse.

7. Open Home Setup.
   - Initialize the roadmap.
   - Mark one item as bought.
   - Postpone a comfort item.
   - Explain that this prevents early lifestyle creep.

8. Activate Mode.
   - Activate an adaptive mode, such as Aggressive Saving.
   - Show guidance and the active mode on the dashboard.

9. Open Transport.
   - Add public transport, Uber/Didi, motorcycle, and mixed options.
   - Evaluate transport fit.
   - Show burden, risk, current recommendation, and future viable option.

10. Open Meals.
    - Request suggestions based on craving, effort, budget, time, and equipment.
    - Explain that food decisions are part of independence, not a side feature.

11. Return to Dashboard.
    - Show the updated command center.
    - Point at recent decisions and the next action.

## 5. Closing - 15 seconds

"This is not a budgeting app. It's a decision system."

TranquiloOS shows how product thinking, domain rules, and full-stack engineering can turn a vague life decision into an explainable workflow.
